Deep Learning-Based Image and Video Compression: A List of Recent Publications

Readme

This is a list of recent publications regarding deep learning-based image and video compression. The list is maintained by the USTC-FVC research team (USTC is short for the University of Science and Technology of China, and FVC stands for Future Video Coding). Please contact Dong Liu if you want to add your publications into the list or if you want to send us a reminder about missing publications.
We have written an overview paper about deep image/video compression: arXiv. This page contains new publications only, the old publications that have been included in the overview paper do not appear in this page.
Last updated on September 28, 2020 by Runyu Yang.
